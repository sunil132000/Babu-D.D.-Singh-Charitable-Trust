Welcome to Babu D.D. Singh Charitable Trust

Babu D.D. Singh Charitable Trust is a non-profit, non-political organization working in an inclusive way for the holistic development of children and persons with and without disabilities, and from marginalized sections of the society.

Who We Are

Babu D.D. Singh Charitable Trust is dedicated to enabling differently abled and marginalized children and youth to receive comprehensive rehabilitation, education, and vocational training. Our mission is to empower these individuals so they can take care of their social, physical, and spiritual needs, and become fully integrated into society.

We envision a world that ensures the inclusion of differently abled and marginalized children and youth into mainstream society. Our aim is to create an environment where they feel welcome, supported, and empowered.

Our Initiatives

Health

Babu D.D. Singh Charitable Trust holistic rehab program includes Medical, Therapeutics, Capacity Building, and Sensitization.

Education

Welcome to Babu D.D. Singh Charitable Trust, education is a vital step in supporting the confidence, dreams, and talents of our students.

Livelihood

We empower youth with disabilities through skills training and aiding micro-enterprise establishment.

Higher Education

Babu D.D. Singh Charitable Trust Higher Education division empowers Persons with Disabilities through education, employment, and awareness.
